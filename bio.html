Short Bio

Kevin grew up in Westfield, NJ. Growing up, Kevin enjoying playing sports such as soccer, basketball, lacrosse, tennis, and baseball. Ultimately, he would wind up playing primarily baseball as he progressed through intermediate school and high school. In addittion to sports, Kevin also enjoyed playing the trombone. He played in many different bands and groups of many different genres ranging anywhere from jazz and funk to orchetral and classical.

After high school, Kevin continues to play baseball for the Tufts University Jumbos. Between practice and games, Kevin studies Quantitative Economics in addition to Computer Science and Finance. Outside of baseball and class, Kevin is a member of the Delta Upsilon fraternity.
